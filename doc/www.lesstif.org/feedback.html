Feedback

We are really interested in your opinion about our project (and our webpages perhaps ;-)
Send any questions or comments regarding the LessTif software package and this website to the mailing list at lesstif-discuss@lists.sourceforge.net

However if you are going to report a specific bug in our recent distributions, consider to use our SF bug tracking facility for this. Advantage is that this creates an entry in a database which prevents the report from being forgotten, even if it can't be addressed immediately ...

Also please read the helpful comments in bugs.html and, of course, our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)!
More information (e.g. how to subscribe) about our mailinglists can be found on another page.
