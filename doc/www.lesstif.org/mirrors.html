Mirror Sites for LessTif

Mirrors are good. They help in our never-ending quest to conserve bandwidth.
So please use a mirror next to your location to retrieve packages from our FTP repository.

Note the we do check those links from time to time, but it will always happen that one or more sites of that list are not available.
