Mailinglists

LessTif currently has two public mailing lists: lesstif, and lesstif-commits. Those are used to keep in touch, solve problems, and track development.

LessTif Public Mailing Lists
lesstif-discuss - at - lists.sourceforge.net is the list for general discussion of LessTif.
Visit http://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/lesstif-discuss to subscribe.
lesstif-commits shows CVS commits (and is therefore read-only).
Visit http://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/lesstif-commits to subscribe.

Mailing list archives
Both lesstif and lesstif-commits/ are archived online.
